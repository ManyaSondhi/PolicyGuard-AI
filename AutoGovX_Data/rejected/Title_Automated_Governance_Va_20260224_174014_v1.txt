Title: Automated Governance Validation Using Transformer Models

Abstract:
This research guarantees improved scientific review consistency through automated policy enforcement mechanisms. The proposed system was tested on a dataset of 8,000 validated research submissions from interdisciplinary journals.

Methodology:
The framework integrates structured claim extraction with dataset-driven validation scoring. The dataset includes labeled examples for novelty and compliance evaluation. Experimental analysis compares performance against traditional review workflows.

Results:
The system demonstrates stable evaluation performance and improved alignment with predefined governance standards.

Conclusion:
The framework provides a scalable infrastructure for intent-aware scientific governance.
